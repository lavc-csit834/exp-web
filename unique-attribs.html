    Unique attributes of Linux OS

  • It is UNIX(like) for PC.
  • Licensing is Open Source – GNU GPL.
  • GNU = GNU NOT UNIX (recursive ad infinitum).
  • Linux uses GNU General Public License (GPLv2) developed by Free Software Foundation (FSF). Google on Richard Stallman (GPLv1). Lawrence Lessig (GPLv2, 3). Make sure you visit fsf.org and study the philosophy.
  • Any source code under GPL must be freely available, when modified and distributed it must also be freely available keeping the software free.
  • OSS (Open Source Software) – expound on open systems: Discussion.
  • Pros and cons to open and closed systems – ask the students.
