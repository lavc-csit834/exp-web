    A very brief history of Linux

    Linux is based on UNIX which was created by Ken Thompson and Dennis Ritchie (co-creator of C programming language) of AT&T Bell Labs in 1969. See: History of Linux - Wikipedia

  • Motivation was to have a UNIX like operating system to run on PCs free of charge.
  • Why UNIX? Because it’s the best Operating System.
  • Linus Torvald wrote the OS in 1991(in its elementary version) posted it on the Internet, and a bandwagon started and now it’s a major force in the OS market.
  • Linux is a very large universe, just google “linux” and see what you come up with.
